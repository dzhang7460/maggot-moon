The Great Motherland Announces Purity Initiative to Strengthen National Unity

Zone Two, July 6, 1956 — By Comrade Viktor Melnov, Senior Correspondent for National Advancement

Time and time again, the Motherland has proved itself to be the most presiding nation amongst the greatest of powers. Despite this, our land has recently been experiencing internal tensions which have been significantly taking a toll on the future of this nation and our unity.

Recent reports of confusion and uncooperative behaviour in Zone Seven have prompted renewed efforts to guide citizens toward clarity and national pride. At times like this with such conflation and ambiguity, we must acknowledge and bring forth the preeminent leadership of our very own, the High Council of the Motherland and have faith in the Mothers for Purity.

Following these unprecedented events, the High Council has introduced the Purity Initiative, a nationwide program designed to reaffirm loyalty and strengthen unity among all zones.

Throughout this program, our most trusted greenflies will cleanse the streets of this land by doing what's best for our lost brothers who have fallen into this mischievous trap. For ensured succession of this, all heads must look at one path to endeavor the goals of our Motherland.

“Purity is not perfection, but purpose. It’s the will of every citizen aligning with the will of the Motherland.” — Mr. Gunnell, Zone 7 Teacher

Officials reminded the public that questioning the Motherland's direction only delays collective progress. “Those who trust, build, and those who doubt, destroy” declared Chairman Gantz during today’s broadcast.

Leaders across Zone Two and Zone Three whole heartedly welcome the initiative, whilst highlighting gains in production, education, and community morale. Citizens are encouraged to participate in weekly meetings celebrating the triumphs of the Motherland.
