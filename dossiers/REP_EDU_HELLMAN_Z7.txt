SCHOOL DISCIPLINE MEMORANDUM

---

Respected Motherland,

It is with a sense of duty that I report on the condition of several pupils under my authority. Despite instruction in the approved curriculum, a number of them are failing to meet the proper standards expected of disciplined and capable young citizens. Their difficulty in mastering the lessons and maintaining the required discipline suggests troubling weaknesses.
Such shortcomings, if left uncorrected, could threaten the development of strong and loyal members of society. I therefore recommend firm guidance and correction so that these pupils may be properly shaped according to the standards of the Motherland.

With loyal respect,
From Headmaster Hellman
