CITIZEN VIGILANCE SUBMISSION

---

Dear Motherland,

I humbly bring to your attention a matter that has caused me concern within my neighborhood. A nearby family conducts themselves in a manner that seems unusual compared to the pride and openness shown by loyal citizens. They rarely participate in community celebrations, keep themselves isolated, and do not appear to display the enthusiasm and devotion expected when the voice of the Motherland is heard.
Because the safety and purity of our society depend upon vigilance, I feel it is my duty to report this behavior. It raises the possibility that something improper may be concealed within that household. I trust that the Motherland will judge the matter with wisdom and strength.

In faithful service,
From Mrs. Fielder (Mothers for Purity, Zone 7)
