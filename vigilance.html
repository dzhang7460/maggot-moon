Zone Seven Student Awarded Highest Honor for Patriotic Vigilance

By Anya Rostova, Ministry of Education Attaché | ZONE SEVEN, July 15, 1956 —

The High Council awarded fifteen-year-old Hans Fielder the Golden Star of Purity yesterday for identifying and reporting subversive elements within his local school district.

Fielder, the Head Perfect of Zone Seven Secondary, successfully led a coalition of students to monitor peers who displayed uncooperative behaviour, physical impurities, or a failure to salute. His intelligence reports directly resulted in the prompt removal of several unfit students and faculty members over the past term.

“Purity requires constant vigilance against those with physical and mental defects. Hans Fielder represents the ideal future of the Motherland. He enforces discipline when weak individuals attempt to disrupt our social order.” — Headmaster Hellman, Morning Assembly
